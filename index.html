Our Mission

Providing Shelter and Care for Abandoned Elderly and Mentally Challenged

The journey of **Manav Seva Ashram (MSA) in Swaroopganj, Rajasthan**, began with a simple, yet profound, realization: the profound loneliness and suffering of abandoned elders and mentally challenged individuals on our streets. We exist to restore **dignity**, provide **unconditional support**, and help reconnect lost souls with their families.

Values: Compassion, Service, Respect, and Humanity.

Our Impact: Lifelong Care, Medical Support, and Family Reconnection Services
